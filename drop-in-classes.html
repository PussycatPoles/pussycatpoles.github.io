Open Practice Sessions – Maidstone

Ready to level up your training? Our Open Practice Sessions are perfect for anyone who has completed at least a beginner course and wants some extra time in the air (or on the pole)!

These relaxed, self-led sessions give you the space to practice the moves you've been learning in class, build strength, improve technique, and gain confidence at your own pace. It’s a great way to keep progressing and have fun doing it!

While these aren’t instructor-led classes, there will always be a friendly instructor around if you have any questions or need a quick spot.

Prices:

  • £5 if you're currently enrolled in a course
  • £10 for non-members

Session Times:

  • Tuesdays – 6:30pm (Pole & Circus)
  • Thursdays – 6:30pm & 7:30pm (Circus)
  • Fridays – 6:30pm (Pole & Circus)
  • Saturdays – 10:30am (Pole & Circus)

Whether you’re perfecting a pose or just want some extra playtime on your favorite apparatus, these sessions are your chance to shine!
