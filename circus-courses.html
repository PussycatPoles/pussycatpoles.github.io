Circus Courses

Discover the magic of aerial hoop, silks, and trapeze in our beginner-friendly circus courses!

Hoop Dreams: Beginner Aerial Hoop Course – Level 1

Ever wanted to feel like you're flying? Welcome to the world of aerial hoop (also known as lyra)—a beautiful steel circle that hangs in the air and lets you spin and pose like a true circus star!

In this 8-week beginner course, you’ll learn all the basics: how to get on and off the hoop safely, move around it with confidence, and start building the strength and flexibility you'll need for more advanced tricks down the line.

No experience? No problem. This class is made for absolute beginners, and we’ll be cheering you on every step of the way. Bring your camera—you’ll want to capture your gorgeous new hoop skills!

8-week course | £120.00

What’s Next?

After completing Level 1, continue your aerial journey in our follow-on courses! As you gain strength and confidence, you'll unlock new tricks, transitions, and dynamic moves. Keep climbing—literally and figuratively!

Soar with Silks: Beginner Aerial Silks Course – Level 1

Ready to climb, twist, and fly? Aerial silks let you defy gravity and create stunning shapes high above the ground!

In this 8-week beginner course, we’ll teach you the basics—how to wrap, pose, and move safely on the silks. You’ll build strength, balance, and confidence through static poses and creative movement.

8-week course | £120.00

Keep Flying…

Once you've completed Level 1, you'll be ready to level up and learn even more! Expect jaw-dropping drops, beautiful sequences, and a major sense of achievement as you rise to new heights.

Swing Into Flight: Beginner Static Trapeze Course – Level 1

Step into the spotlight with static trapeze—a classic circus apparatus that combines strength, grace, and fun!

In this 8-week beginner course, you’ll learn the fundamentals: how to mount and dismount, find your balance, and explore poses and sequences on and under the bar.

8-week course | £120.00

Your Aerial Adventure Awaits…

After mastering the basics, keep climbing and swinging in Level 2 and beyond. Each course adds new tricks and transitions that keep you inspired and growing.
