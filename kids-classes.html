Kids Aerial Circus Classes

Ages 7+ | Tuesdays @ 5:30pm & Thursdays @ 5:00pm

Ready to flip, fly, and have FUN? Our Kids Aerial Classes are the perfect way for kids who love to climb, spin, and explore the exciting world of circus skills! Each session blends aerial hoop, silks, and silk sling—so there’s always something new to try and master.

Over 8 action-packed weeks, kids will learn to climb, swing, spin, and pose in the air—all while building strength, confidence, coordination, and creativity. It’s an incredible full-body workout, disguised as serious fun!

Class Details:

  • For kids aged 7 and up
  • 45-minute classes
  • Tuesdays at 5:30pm & Thursdays at 5:00pm
  • £80 for 8-week course or £15 for a single session

Whether they’re future circus stars or just want to try something new, our aerial classes are a fantastic way to stay active and make new friends—all while flying high!
