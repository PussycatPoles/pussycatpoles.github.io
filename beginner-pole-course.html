Level 1 – Beginner Course

Absolutely no experience needed – just bring your curiosity and a can-do attitude. This 8-week beginner course is your first step into the world of pole—and it's made just for you.

You’ll start with the absolute basics, learning simple but seriously impressive techniques like spins, transitions, and strength-building exercises. We’ll also gently introduce the foundations of climbing and inverting—no pressure, just progress at your own pace.

Each week, you’ll build confidence as your body gets stronger, your flexibility improves, and your self-belief starts to soar.

What You’ll Learn:

  • Basic spins & grips
  • Simple transitions & floorwork
  • Strength-building moves to prepare for climbing & inverting
  • Flexibility and core training
  • Movement flow & confidence on the pole

No need to bring anything—just bring yourself and get ready to feel unstoppable.
