Level X

Move Top Tip Progression

Super Girl

 From a climb, regrip the legs so that the pole is placed high in the thighs... From the Super Girl position, try to lower down slowly into a hangback & handstand to dismount.

Sideways Climb

 Place the back of your strong leg tightly on the pole about hip height... You can finish this climb with a French Kiss, or create your own move when you are there!

The Mushroom

 From the French Kiss, bend the bottom/straight leg over the top of the other leg... Play with the transition from French Kiss into the Mushroom and then back again.

Shouldermount

 Stand with your back on the pole and place the hands on in a cup grip above your head... Take this into an upside-down no hands or a Pole Cat.

Half Butterfly

 From the Caroline, slide the bottom hand down the pole so you are in a split grip... To perfect this pose â€“ Create a nice arch by extending your free foot back towards your head.

Angel

 From a Star, make sure you have a secure grip in the thigh and the back of your shoulder... Experiment with different arm positions; try taking hold of your legs with the opposite hands.

Super Man

 From an upside down, back of the knee grip, raise your torso so you are able to place your strong hand... Aim to make the hip twist transition smooth & extend the free arm. The body should be in a pretty horizontal pose.

Climb Into Upside Down

 From a climb, ensure both hands are on the pole at head height... Aim to do this move without using the pole to push off from. Try climbing into a helicopter and then straightening the legs.

Extended Butterfly

 From a Half Butterfly position, place the bottom hand down the pole in an upside-down grip... Arch the back & extend the leg as far as you can but make sure you control your dismount.

Climbing Flag

 Re-visit The Flag from Advanced Plus. This time add on a climb beforehand... Experiment with leg positions as in the Advanced Plus course.

Elbow Grip

 From upside down no hands, take one arm around the pole across the body and ensure... Practice holding this move & finding your balance. Take the legs as wide as you can to perfect it.

Underarm Grab

 Climb the pole and with hands at head height, bring your body to one side... Experiment with different leg positions.

Full Butterfly

 From a Pole Cat, re-grip the hand nearest to your belly button to place your elbow around the pole... Link this with a move of your choice.

Box Splits

 From Half Splits, re-grip the bottom hand so it is placed just underneath your top hand... Try to take the legs as wide as possible, but listen to your body and know your limits.

Fireman Pull Up Spin

 As you spin and lift into this move, place one foot in front of the pole, and one behind... Try and do 2 climbs to the top. Point your toes & lift your knees high to get the height.
